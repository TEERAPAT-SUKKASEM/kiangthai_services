Delete your Kiang Thai Service account

How to remove your account and what data is deleted.

This page describes how users of the Kiang Thai Service mobile app can delete their account and associated data, in line with Google Play's Data Safety requirements.

Delete from inside the app

  1. Open the Kiang Thai Service app and sign in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap Profile Settings.
  4. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm in the dialog. Your account and data are deleted immediately and you are signed out.

What is deleted

  • Your authentication record (email, password hash, session tokens).
  • Your profile (full name, phone number, saved addresses, role).
  • All bookings you created as a customer or were assigned as a technician.
  • All chat messages you sent in any booking conversation.
  • Push notification tokens registered to your account.

What is retained

We do not retain personal data after deletion. Anonymized or aggregated records that contain no identifier linking back to you (for example, service-type counts used for internal reporting) may remain. Backups are overwritten on our provider's standard rotation schedule.

This action is permanent. Deleted bookings, messages, and profile information cannot be recovered. If you have an active booking, please cancel or complete it before deleting your account.
